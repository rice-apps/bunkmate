Welcome to...

Hello Detectives! On behalf of the entire team, we would like to welcome you to McMurtry

College! We are James, Prerna, and Vidal, your 2022 MysteriO-Week Coordinators, and we

could not be more excited to introduce you to the mystery that we call Rice University! It

is a major accomplishment to get in, and you should be extremely proud of yourselves. We

want to say congratulations, as this is a sign of all of your hard work paying off. We are so

proud of your resilience and are so excited to join you on this next journey. We know that

this is the start of your academic and social career as you continue to chase your goals

and ambitions. Also, we would like to congratulate you on being the 14th matriculating

class at McMurtry College! We are thrilled to welcome a new round of Murts into our com- munity and can’t wait to see you represent McMurtry and Rice all over campus.

In these next few years of your life, you’ll be working hard, studying, meeting new friends,

and enjoying yourself. The time will be filled with plenty of opportunities as well as chal- lenges for you to face and overcome. It’s going to require you to dig deep, ask questions,

study an extra hour (or 2!), and maybe stay up late a couple of nights. But do not be

dismayed! You are not on this journey alone. You are going to be surrounded by other

students, professors, and Advisors who have been in your shoes before and can help you

get the resources that you need for you to succeed. We have been preparing these last 9

months in order for you to be integrated into Rice and McMurtry smoothly, so we hope

that MysteriO-Week creates an environment where you can open up and really get to

know each other well.
